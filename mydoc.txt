Based on your Great Library documents, **Sakura Miyamoto** is a fictional character with the following profile:

## Basic Information
* **Age:** 17 years old
* **Occupation:** High school student at Hikari Academy and part-time shrine maiden
* **Setting:** Neo-Tokyo, year 2145

## Background
Sakura was born into a family of traditional shrine keepers and works at the ancient Moonlight Shrine, which has stood for over 800 years. She balances her modern student life with her traditional duties at the temple.

## Appearance
* Shoulder-length black hair with distinctive silver streaks (appeared on her 16th birthday)
* Unusual amber eyes that glow faintly in the dark
* Wears a navy blazer with red tie (school uniform) or traditional white and red shrine maiden robes

## Personality
* Determined and compassionate
* Strong sense of justice
* Known for standing up to bullies and helping classmates
* Fiercely loyal but occasionally stubborn
* Playful sense of humor (loves terrible puns)

## Abilities
* Can see and communicate with spirits (inherited gift from maternal lineage)
* Can create protective barriers using spiritual energy
* Trained in spirit channeling since childhood
* Powers still developing and can activate unpredictably when emotional

## Key Relationships
* **Kenji Tanaka** - Best friend and tech genius who helps research supernatural phenomena
* **Priestess Himeko** - Her grandmother and spiritual mentor
* **Akira Sato** - Rival exorcist from a competing shrine

## Main Goal
To uncover the truth behind mysterious spirit disturbances in Neo-Tokyo and protect both the human and spirit worlds from an awakening ancient threat.
